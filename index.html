Frequently Asked Questions

Is this extension free?

+

Yes! All features are completely free. There are no subscriptions, no hidden costs, and no usage limits. We believe clarity should be accessible to everyone.

Does it work on all websites?

+

Yes, the extension works on virtually any website. Simply right-click or click the extension icon, and it will explain the current page. It works on news sites, documentation, blogs, company pages, and more.

How fast is it?

+

Most explanations appear in 2-5 seconds. For pages you've already explained, results are instant thanks to our smart caching system.

Can I explain just selected text?

+

Yes! Simply highlight text on any page, right-click, and select "Explain selected text" from the context menu. This is perfect for understanding specific sections without explaining the entire page.

What's the difference between ELI5, Business, and Technical modes?

+

ELI5: Simple, friendly explanations perfect for anyone. Uses plain language and analogies.

Business: Professional perspective focusing on what it does, who it's for, and why it matters.

Technical: Detailed breakdowns for developers and experts, preserving technical accuracy while explaining.

Is my data safe?

+

Absolutely. We don't track your browsing, collect personal information, or store data on external servers. Explanation history is stored locally on your device only. We only process the visible text content of pages you explicitly request explanations for.

Does it read my passwords?

+

No, never. The extension only reads visible text content from webpages. It cannot and does not access passwords, login credentials, or any sensitive information. Your privacy and security are our top priorities.

Do I need to create an account?

+

No account required! Just install the extension and start using it immediately. No sign-up, no email, no hassle.

What if the explanation is still too complex?

+

Click the "Too complex" button, and we'll regenerate the explanation with even simpler language, shorter sentences, and more basic analogies.

Can I export explanations?

+

Yes! You can copy explanations to your clipboard, and we're working on additional export options like Markdown and PDF.
